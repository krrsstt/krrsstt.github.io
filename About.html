About Us


Awesome Travels has been established in 2023 by 4 friends who love to travel. We want to offer people easy and effortless way to plan a vacation.


Idea to this business started when two of us was asked to plan a vacation to friends who were too busy to do it themselves. They were so happy and enjoyed the vacation so much that we decided to make travel planning our job.


Our Values


Our main and important mission is to make awesome experiences to our customers. We respect green values and always plan our vacation as green as possible. We also favor local entrepreneurers over giant companies.
